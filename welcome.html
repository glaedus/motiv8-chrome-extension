To get started, before clicking on the extension icon and opening the Motiv8 extension, ensure that:
  • you are NOT in incognito mode
  • you have allowed chrome notifications in computer system settings
  • Refer to guide below to enable notifications!

MacOS

1. Click on the apple icon at the top left hand corner of the screen. Click System Preferences

2. Click on 'Notifications & Focus'

3. Under 'Notifications & Focus', scroll down to find Google Chrome. Click 'Allow notifications' and tick the other checkboxes below


Windows 10

1. Press the start button. Click the settings icon as shown below

2. Click 'System'

3. Click on 'Notifications & actions'. Turn on Google Chrome notifications


Windows 11

1. Right-click on the Start icon and select Settings from the list

2. In the System tab, click on Notifications

3. Under 'Notifications from apps and other senders', turn on Google Chrome notifications
